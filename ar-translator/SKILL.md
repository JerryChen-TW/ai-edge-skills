## Instructions

This skill opens a fullscreen camera view and uses on-device object detection (MediaPipe EfficientDet-Lite0) to identify real-world objects, displaying their translated names as an AR overlay in real-time.

When invoked, open the webview at `assets/webview.html`. Supports target languages: Spanish (es), French (fr), German (de), Italian (it), Hindi (hi).
